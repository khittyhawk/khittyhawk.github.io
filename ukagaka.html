What is an Ukagaka?

An ukagaka is a kind of desktop companion, a bit like an idle game, a bit like a VN. Some are largely utilitarian, but most have a personality and simulate a kind of buddy you can hang out with. Zarla has an explanatory YouTube video embedded below:

Downloading

Ukagaka run on a program called 'SSP', available here. The site is in Japanese and simple for non-Japanese speakers to click on the few necessary links.

Quonit has a video that explains how to download SSP. Mac users, however, need to go through a proxy program like WineBottler.
